OSSSC CRE 2026 Notification Details

Odisha Sub-Ordinate Staff Selection Commission (OSSSC) has released the Combined Recruitment Examination (CRE) 2026 for various District Cadre posts. This is a golden opportunity for aspirants looking for Government Jobs in Odisha.

Job Overview

Exam NameOSSSC CRE 2026 (Group B & C)
VacanciesRI, ARI, AMIN, ICDS Supervisor, SFS
Selection ModeDirect Recruitment (Exam Based)
Job LocationAcross Odisha (District Level)
Age Limit21 to 38 Years (As per rules)

Eligibility & Post Details

1. Revenue Inspector (RI): Graduate in any discipline from a recognized University + Computer Knowledge.
2. ICDS Supervisor (Female Only): Graduate in Arts, Science, or Commerce (Social Work, Psychology or Home Science preferred).
3. ARI & AMIN: Must have passed Higher Secondary (+2) Examination or equivalent.
4. Statistical Field Surveyor (SFS): +2 Pass in any stream from CHSE Odisha or equivalent.

Exam Pattern (Expected)

The selection will be based on a Two-Stage process:

  • Stage 1: Preliminary Exam (MCQ) - 100 Marks (Screening Test)
  • Stage 2: Main Written Exam - 180-200 Marks (Final Selection)
  • Practical: Skill Test (Windows, MS Office, Internet) - Qualifying in nature.

* Negative Marking: 0.33 per wrong answer.
